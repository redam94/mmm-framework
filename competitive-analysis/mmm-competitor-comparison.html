How this comparison was built

The subject platform was characterized directly from its source tree — capabilities are marked shipped vs. deferred, not taken from marketing copy. Every competitor profile was researched from primary sources (vendor docs, GitHub/PyPI, technical papers, funding announcements) and then adversarially fact-checked by a second pass, with corrections logged and a confidence grade assigned.

Where a claim originates from a direct competitor's own blog or benchmark, it is flagged competitor-sourced and treated as directional, not settled fact — most notably the head-to-head speed/accuracy numbers PyMC Labs and Recast publish about rivals. Pricing figures for closed vendors (Recast, Mutinex, Analytic Partners, Kantar/Ipsos) are third-party estimates, not quotes; the vendors publish no price lists.

Ratings compress a nuanced landscape into a four-step scale for scanning — read the per-dimension notes and profiles for the real texture. This is a point-in-time snapshot (mid-2026): open-source versions, funding, and analyst positions all drift.
